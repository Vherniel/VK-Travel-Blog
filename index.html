Welcome to V&K Travel Blogs

Welcome to V&K Travel Blog. We're creating this blog to tell stories from our travels around the Philippines between year 2015 and 2018.

Our first trip around the Philippines was back in 2015 when we backpacked from Malabon to Boracay. The trip was badly planned and poorly researched but it was the greatest time of our life!

We aim to provide an honest and interesting journal detailing our travels, our thoughts and our regrets and all the fun and exciting moments in between.

Enjoy reading!
